The Data Professional

As the 4th industrial revolution and 4th paradigm of science unfolds with the generation of data at unprecedented levels, it has become ever more important to create value from this big data. While creating value from big data, data scientists act as the key professional in this transformation and need to comply with the regulations on data use, as well as being mindful of individual privacy and, equally, the ethical use and reuse of data.

Deciphering Big Data

This module explores the process of data wrangling from collection to publishing and the ability to identify resources to undertake the data wrangling process. These processes are essential because it improves the quality of data by transforming the data collected in the raw state to a more usable format for a variety of applications by eliminating unnecessary duplications and redundancies of data items through processes such as cleansing and normalisation.
